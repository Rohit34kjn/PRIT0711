About PRIT PURE

Prem Se Shuddh Tak - Our Journey of Pure Fragrance

Our Story

PRIT PURE AGARBATTI was born with a simple mission - to bring pure, natural fragrance to every home. We believe that true devotion starts with purity, and that's why every stick is hand-rolled with care by skilled artisans.

Located in the heart of Silapathar, Assam, we combine traditional methods with quality ingredients to create incense sticks that are 100% natural and chemical-free.
